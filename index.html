About me

Throughout my 3 years of university, I have developed various skills in which can help me achieve my goals in field of research. I am keen to start work within the game industry and to expand upon my knowledge in other languages. The primarily languages I have used are C# and the unreal engines blueprints, I have touched upon C++ and JavaScript.

Repulsive Dreams

Repulsive Dreams was a second-year project I did, it is a third person horror game that contains, a HUD, AI path finding and a complex inventory system. The player navigates though an old mansion, finding clues on what happened and picking up various items, such as weapons, health items and keys to unlock the many doors within the mansion.

Language used: • Unreal engine • Blueprints

Endless Runner

This mobile game is based upon the many endless runner games around, I created this small game as part of my second-year course and finished it during my own time. Though a mobile game, the gameplay video is played on a computer, but with the touch controls assign to the mouse.

Language used: • Unreal engine • Blueprints
